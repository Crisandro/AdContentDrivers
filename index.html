1st Station :
Balloon Pyramid
- Aim of the game is for each team to form a pyramid of cups using only a balloon, their hands must be behind their back. The team must form a stacked pyramid of at least 6 cups, this is achieved with the balloon in their mouth; whilst in their mouth they put the balloon in the cup and inflate the balloon to secure the cup. They then move the cup over to their target table and put it on the table by deflating the balloon. The next person in the team does the next cup and so on. After completing the challenge proceed to the next station .


2nd Station :
Pick up Sticks
All player begins the game by holding all of the sticks in their hand together and then dropping them to the ground, scattering them across the floor. Each player then proceeds to pick up the sticks, one by one, without moving any sticks other than the stick they are attempting to pick up. If a stick is accidentally/intentionally moved they should start from the beginning until all sticks are picked up. After completing the challenge proceed to the next station.


3rd Station :
Pick up coins
The team must answer a math problem from the box given by the assigned station facilitator and then collects coins from the pool with the sum equal to the answer of the answered math problem Give the collected coins to the assigned station facilitator to sum up the coins and to verify the correct answer. If the answer is incorrect the team must pick up another math problem from the box and start answering again. After completing the challenge proceed to the next station.


4th Station :
Water Relay Challenge
The team must make a line with a bucket of water and an empty bucket on each end of the line. All members must face the direction where the bucket of water is placed. When the team is ready they must yell "Go !" so that the assigned station facilitator will give the sponge to the first person in the line , they must dip the sponge on the water bucket and hand it to the next person in line, but only by passing it over their head. The second person in line must pass the item on by passing it under their legs. Each time the object reaches the last person, have them squeeze as much water out of the sponge as possible into the empty bucket, then run to the front of the line and be the new head of the line.
